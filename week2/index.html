Chocolate Cake

Prep Time: 30 mins

Serving Size: 6 people

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, and baking soda.
  3. Add butter, eggs, and milk. Mix until smooth.
  4. Pour mixture into a greased baking pan.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes. Let it cool.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
